FinDroid Help:

Introduction:

FinDroid is a GPS and Messaging based phone finder application. This application will provide you great help in finding your lost phone. This application will provide you many facilities like the Map and the Radar views to get your lost phone back

NOTE: GPS has to be activated manually. No application has the authority to do so.

Application Features:

  • SEARCH A PHONE:

    This button will let you get the GPS co-ordinates from the lost phone by the means of messaging. You do not have to type the message, the FinDroid will do so for you. This feature requires FinDroid to be installed on the lost phone to communicate. After pressing this button the application will ask for the lost phone's contact number and its FinDroid password. Please do provide the details and press OK. This will send a message to the lost phone and wait till the co-ordinates are re-lied back to us.

  • Resume:

    This menu button will launch the map view and the radar view. The map view shows the lost phones location as well as your location. It also has several options for navigating and ringing the phone from there directly.

  • Change Pasword:

Change the FinDroid password

  • Settings:

Opens the preferences activity where you can customize the way you want this application to work.

How does it work?

  • Once FinDroid is activated, it will monitor incoming messages for the keywords. (You can set keywords of your own choice for alerting FinDroid)

  • The keywords will tell FinDroid to perform specific actions. They may be sending the GPS co-ordinates or ringing the phone.

  • After confirming the keyword, FinDroid performs the required action.

How does the search feature works?

  • The search feature requires FinDroid installed on the lost phone.

  • On clicking the 'SEARCH A PHONE' button, user should enter the phone number and FinDroid password of the lost phone (The password is required so that no unknown person can track your location).

  • After entering the details, FinDroid will send an SMS to the lost phone for getting the GPS co-ordinates. NOTE: The time taken by FinDroid to get the GPS location may vary on different phones.

  • The lost phone will then send you back the GPS co-ordinates after confirming the password.

  • On receiving the GPS co-ordinates, the FinDroid application will provide you map and radar views to track your lost phone.

How does the sim card change detection works?

  • Once this feature is activated, FinDroid will check for unwanted sim card change on every boot.

  • If the inserted sim card is not registered with FinDroid, it will get the sim's info and the GPS location and will send it to the user defined phone number

  • You can change the alert phone number and register sim card in the settings.

FinDroid permissions explained:

  • SEND_SMS:

For sending the GPS location to the required number.

  • RECEIVE_SMS:

For monitoring incoming messages for FinDroid keywords.

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION:

To get the GPS location.

  • INTERNET:

To view the map in the map activity.

  • READ_PHONE_STATE:

To read the sim card details.

  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED:

To check if the phone is booted, so that sim card change can be detected.
